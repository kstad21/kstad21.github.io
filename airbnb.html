Purpose:

This was part of a class at UCSD, Data Science in Practice. I worked in a group of three, which required collaboration, clear deadlines, and honest communication. The goal of this project was to investigate a question through data cleaning and exploratory data analysis, with a final analysis at the end.

Ideas:

We wondered how big of an impact location would have on the rating of an Airbnb, versus other factors like rooms and amenities.

Execution:

I cleaned a few of our datasets (Airbnb reviews and listings, neighborhood data, income data) and performed EDA on them using Pandas, seaborn, and matplotlib. Going through all of our visualizations, we found that Airbnb's ratings on a scale of 1-5 make it difficult to look at differences in ratings because they're all clustered so tightly at the top. We still found that location was not a good predictor for how well an Airbnb would be rated.
