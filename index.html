About Me

Hello! I'm a third-year Cognitive Science and Machine Learning student at UCSD. With over 4 years of Computer Science tutoring, including tutoring for UCSD's CSE department, I am passionate about increasing accessibility to CS education.

I am fascinated by how we think, particularly the way neuromodulation guides our memories and learning through the strengthening and weakening of connections, and the ways we can computationally model what's happening in the brain.

I love coding and have a mix of self-taught and formal education in Java, Python, C, Javascript, HTML, and CSS.

Recently, I discovered a passion for analyzing and visualizing data, leading me to gain experience with Python libraries like seaborn, pandas, scikit-learn, numpy, and matplotlib.

In my free time, I love to watch football and basketball (Go Niners & Warriors <3). Check out my projects for a mix of my research experience and school, work, and personal projects.
