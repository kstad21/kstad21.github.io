Purpose:

Provide a more centralized space for our tutoring center's information. Make searching for tutor appointments or attributes much faster and less labor intensive, rather than having to manually look through many different Google sheets.

Ideas:

I realized I could break down our center into different objects as long as I understood the relationships between them. For example, our whole organization is a Center, which has Tutors that have certain attributes. Tutors have multiple Sessions, which also have different attributes (like subjects and times). These all work together to create an environment in which tutors take appointments inside the Content Tutoring center.

Execution:

Starting with the most simple functions, I started by defining the different objects and their relationships, then built in more and more functionality. To make things more useful, I added ways to import information through csvs, including attendance so that my appointment lookups would be up-to-date. Finally, I created a Main so that users could use the tool through the command line. Currently, I'm working on the front end and learning how to connect my backend Java to a front-end webpage.
